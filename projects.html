JedActivities Application

JedActivities application is an application that provides diverse activities in Jeddah, allowing individuals to engage in various sports and hobbies. All activities are group-oriented to enhance effective community interaction, and understanding of others' traditions, and contribute to reducing stress, tension, and obesity in the community. Reward points we designed this feature to encourage users to remain loyal to the platform and continue booking activities. It leverages the psychological satisfaction of earning rewards to build a stronger connection between the user and the services provided in our application.

Ensanya Application

Ensanya embodies a fundamental tenet of our faith, emphasizing kindness towards all and fostering a spirit of mutual assistance. The core objective of the Ensanya application lies in its mission to forge connections aimed at enhancing lives and alleviating suffering. By facilitating the donation of surplus clothes and furniture, Ensanya seeks to channel resources towards those in need, thereby fostering compassion and solidarity within our communities.
